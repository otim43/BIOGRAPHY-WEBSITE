Who is Keith?

I live in Seeta-Kigunga, Mukono and my home district is Manafwa. I am a lover of choral music and I do train choirs and sing with them in my free time. I sing with Glorious Queen Choir Seeta and All Saints Catholic Choir Seeta all at St. Augustine Seeta Catholic Parish.

What I do

I am currently a student at Victoria University doing business computing with hopes of earning a living out of my computer skills when applied in the business world. I do computer repair and maintenance, painting and secretarial services to make ends meet. I currently work with Samanga Solution Ltd. as a free-lance technician and I can offer the services to anyone that needs them as I am just a phone call way. I also do music training for choirs in schools, churches and all organizations that need the service at a fee that is negotiable. For all those that need my services, you can reach me via email, send email or direct call on +256 777222008.

Me and My community

In the community in which I live, I always spend my evening and free time with my fellow choristers at church teaching them how to sing better. I was also chosen as the leader of all choirs at the parish give my ability to train and teach music.
